Call for Abstracts

The 18th ANSHM 2026 Conference invites submissions of abstracts from researchers and practitioners working in structural health monitoring and related fields.

Abstract Submission Guidelines

  • Abstracts should be written in English and should not exceed 300 words.
  • The abstract should clearly summarise the objectives, methodology, and key contributions of the work.
  • All authors’ full names, affiliations, and email addresses must be included.
  • The presenting author should be clearly indicated.
  • All submissions must be completed through the Microsoft CMT system. Email submissions will not be accepted.

Submission Process (Microsoft CMT)

  • If you do not have a Microsoft CMT account, please create one at: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/
  • Sign in to your Microsoft CMT account.
  • Search for the conference: ANSHM 2026 or directly access the submission portal.
  • Navigate to the submission page and click “Create New Submission”.
  • Enter paper title, abstract text, keywords, and author information.
  • Review all information carefully before submitting.
  • Submit your abstract through the CMT system.

Full Paper Submission (Optional)

Authors may optionally submit a full paper to support their abstract submission. Please note that accepted full papers will not be formally published or indexed, but will be included in the conference electronic proceedings for internal distribution and presentation purposes.

  • Full papers must also be submitted via Microsoft CMT.
  • A full paper template will be provided on the conference website (coming soon).
  • If you have already submitted an abstract, please upload your full paper under the same submission in CMT.
  • If you are submitting a full paper directly, please create a new submission in CMT.
  • Please avoid duplicate submissions for the same work.

About ANSHM 2026

The ANSHM 2026 - 18th Australian Network of Structural Health Monitoring Workshop brings together leading researchers and professionals in structural health monitoring.

It promotes collaboration and innovation in smart infrastructure and sensing technologies.

Hosted in Perth, it offers both academic exchange and cultural experience.

Workshop Theme
Listening to Infrastructure:
Transforming Data into Knowledge
ANSHM 2026 highlights the growing role of structural monitoring, sensing technologies, data analytics, artificial intelligence, and digital tools in transforming raw observations into actionable knowledge. This workshop will explore how structural monitoring data can be used to assess infrastructure health, identify risks, support predictive maintenance, improve resilience, and inform smarter infrastructure management.

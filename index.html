About ANSHM 2026 Conference

The 18th Australian Network of Structural Health Monitoring (ANSHM) 2026 Conference brings together leading researchers and professionals in structural health monitoring.

It promotes collaboration and innovation in smart infrastructure and sensing technologies.

Hosted in Perth, it offers both academic exchange and cultural experience.

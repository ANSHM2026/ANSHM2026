DISCOVER PERTH

Perth blends urban energy with natural beauty, where the Swan River meets the Indian Ocean. From golden beaches and wide-open parks to vibrant food districts and cultural spaces, the city offers a relaxed yet sophisticated atmosphere.

Whether you explore Kings Park, enjoy a sunset at Cottesloe Beach, or take a short trip to Swan Valley wineries, Perth provides a unique Western Australian experience.

Plan your stay and explore more at visitperth.com
